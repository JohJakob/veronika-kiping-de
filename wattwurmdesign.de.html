wattwurm design – experiemnt, integrated design

wattwurm design is a self initiated project in which I want to experiment with the time for a design. I want to evaluate whether it is visible that a design was created in one day, one month or half a year. In november 2018 the idea was born when I wondered why I always got scared of projects I had to do in my studies, especially finishing or sticking to them. This experiment moght help me to create some techniques to structure my work or to realize how I work most efficient and fun.

The first attempt is to create a design in 24 hours from two random things – a need and an adjective.

Currently I keep myself busy with my thesis project – which is this 'sticky' project – but I will continue when I need a break.
