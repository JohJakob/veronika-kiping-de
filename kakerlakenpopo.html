kakerlakenpopo – product design

On this site I want to exhibit some of my casual product items that I created for fun and with things I always wanted to try or even needed.

feuerflieger

This is a sugar cone holder for the traditional geman drink "Feurzangenbowle" which is basically a mix of rum, some spices, oranges and mulled wine. The sugar cone will be lit on fire and so the object is made from stainless steel to provide food safety. The shape is a triangle which is bent to hold the shape of the sugar cone. Due to its shape it is easily stackable and the production is material saving.
