    feuerflieger (~fireplane) →

    These are a sugar cone holders for the traditional geman drink "Feurzangenbowle". The objects used for holding the burning sugar cone are made from a stainless steel sheet. They were bend into shape and are stackable.

    • knife block →

    The knife block does protect from but is not hiding the knives. They are hanging in the structure, barely touching de bottom of it. The block itself is made from steel and wood. By keeping the design simple and clean, it can be personalized easily.

    • shelf →

    A small kitchen shelf with the option of hanging things - mugs in my case. I wanted to work with copper pipes and create a hanging shelf with no visible connections. The focus was on the top shelf but it turned out to become a pair.

    • ring →

    This rings design plays with simple shapes and contrasts. The decoration item lies under the actual ring to enhance the importance and beauty of that complete shape rather than layering something on top. I made a circle and an oval one.

    • porcelain bowl →

    The porcelain bows are unglazen on the outside wich enhances the contrast to its inside. The insides form is based on a sphere, from a certain centerpoint the distance stays the same. This was essential for creating the pattern made with a structure for the pen and a potter's wheel.
