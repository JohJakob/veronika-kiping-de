design jahre / design years – integrated deign / since 2019

The Walz, also called Wanderjahre or Tippelei, was in the Middle Ages as a prerequisite to gain the master's degree and to take up the guild, an association of persons of the same estate. Handwerksgesell_ inside had to leave home for at least three years and one day and wandering around the world to meet new masters against food and Logis to work and learn. The wandering served as a professional, social and personal training of the company.

Even today, craft tradesmen go on a hike. Since she is not is more compulsory, the numbers of wandering people have gone far. The Walz as a tradition is still quite mysterious, as an abuse of this Tradition should be avoided. Knowledge is still preferred today conveyed orally. Since 2014, the company hike has been under the motto ‘Wissen. Können. Weitergeben.’ (‘Knowledge. Skills. Sharing.’) to the intangible cultural heritage of Germany.

Even creative professions can be based on the tradition of rolling their knowledge and Can you test and pass on in the free world? What do you need for that? What needs to happen to use the roll as a model, but tradition not to stand in the way? The Walz was once an integral part of the teaching. Invert must this System of today be adapted, especially with regard to the young Occupational branch of design, which redefines itself over and over again? is not just this applied, agile way of working that what integrated design accounts?

I would like to test this in a self-experiment. During this time would like I investigate if companies and agencies of this unused learning and Exchange form open to face, whether interested in the concept of rolling for Creative professions exist and how traditional crafts think about it. To I would like to meet with people of different professions, students, strangers and Locals talk, but also especially with creative people who are already on the way were or are to figure out what their motivation and goal are behind their journey is.

I would like to learn something new, leave my comfort zone and feel so Develop designer. For me, rolling means risk and uncertainty. But how can you do it make it more popular and safer for future students, graduates or professionals - not just from the design - follow this idea to get through Walz to learn, search and find something new?
