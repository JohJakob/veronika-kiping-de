b|r|ett – product design, free project

b|r|ett (german: bett = bed, brett = board) is a bed frame which is easily assembled and disassembled due to its connection with keyhole brackets – no tools are needed, you just hang the boards together. Its width can also be shortened by simply cutting the connecting boards to the desired length. The frame was made from two acacia wood panels (60 cm x 220 cm).
