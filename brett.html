b|r|ett – product design

I designed a bed which is easily assembled and disassembled due to the connection with keyhole brackets — no tools are needed, you just hang the boards together. With b[r]ett you can shorten the bed's width at any time by simply cutting the connecting boards to your desired length. The frame was made from two acacia wood panels (60 cm*220 cm). video (pwd: kaiping)
